Academic Projects

5-bit Adder Development

(Logic Design, Fall 2019)

Developed a 5-Bit Adder; using a combination of 2-input and 3-input NAND gates, to create a 1-Bit Half-Adder, and one 74283 4-Bit Adding chip, to create one 5-Bit Full Adder on a breadboard. Also used Quartus II to create schematic and waveform graph of circuit.

Tools Used:

  • - Wire-cutters
  • - Wires
  • - Two 7400-NAND gate chips
  • - Two 7410-NAND gate chips
  • - One 74283-4 Bit Adding chip
  • - 2 Breadboards
  • - One 7-segment display
  • - Quartus II 9.11 (for simulations)
